UNIT 5: LIKES AND DISLIKES

Lesson 5.1: Sounds and Spellings

We learn the sounds and letters of words about likes and dislikes.


Examples:

a – apple
m – mango
b – banana
c – carrot


Exercise:

1. Say the sound of the first letter of each word:
  a) Apple
  b) Banana
  c) Mango

2. Point to the picture and say the word.

3. Match the letter with the correct word in the table:

LetterWord
aApple
bBanana
mMango

Lesson 5.2: End of Unit Assessment

1. Which fruit do you like? Write the name: _______

2. Circle the correct answer:
  a) I like apple / I like carrot
  b) I like banana / I like mango

3. Say the first letter of each word: Apple, Mango, Banana.
