UNIT 3: PEOPLE AT HOME



Lesson 1: Introducing family members

We have people who live with us at home. They are our family.

Examples of family members:

father, mother, brother, sister, grandmother, grandfather



Examples:

This is my father.
This is my mother.
These are my brothers.



Short dialogue:

Teacher: Who is he?
Child: He is my father.

Teacher: Who is she?
Child: She is my sister.



Exercise:

1. Write the names:
a) My mother's name is ____________.
b) My father's name is ____________.

2. Draw and label two family members.
3. Say three family members aloud.


Lesson 2: My school

We study at school. We learn many things.

At school we have classrooms, teachers, and friends.



Exercise:

1. Write the name of your school: ____________.

2. Who is your teacher? ____________.
3. Who is your headteacher? ____________.


Lesson 3: People at school – Who is he? Who is she? Who are they?

We use he for a boy or a man.
We use she for a girl or a woman.
We use they for many people.



Examples:

He is the headteacher.
She is the nurse.
They are students.



Exercise:

1. Write: he / she / they
a) The teacher is a woman. ____________
b) The boys are in class. ____________
c) The doctor is a man. ____________


Lesson 4: Sounds and spellings

We read and spell words correctly.



Examples:

f – father
m – mother
s – sister
b – brother



Exercise:

Say and spell:

1. father _______
2. sister _______
3. brother _______
