UNIT 9: WHAT WE DO EVERY DAY

Lesson 9.1: What do you do in the morning?

We talk about morning activities.


  • Wake up
  • Brush teeth
  • Wash face
  • Eat breakfast
  • Go to school

Short dialogue:

Teacher: What do you do in the morning?
Child: I wake up and brush my teeth.

Teacher: Good! What else?
Child: I eat breakfast.
