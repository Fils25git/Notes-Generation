UNIT 4: CLOTHES AND PARTS OF MY BODY



Lesson 1: Clothes that we wear

We wear clothes to cover our bodies.

Examples of clothes:

  • shirt
  • skirt
  • trousers
  • dress
  • shoes
  • hat


Exercise:

1. Point to your shirt.
2. Spell the word shoes ____________.
3. List three clothes you wear.


Lesson 2: Parts of my body

Our body has many parts.

  • head
  • eyes
  • ears
  • mouth
  • hand
  • leg
  • foot


Short dialogue:

Teacher: Touch your head.
Child: (Touches head)

Teacher: Point to your eyes.
Child: (Points to eyes)



Exercise:

Match the body part:

1. I hear with my ________.
2. I see with my ________.
3. I walk with my ________.


Lesson 3: Sounds and spellings

We learn sounds to help us read words.

h – head
l – leg
f – foot
e – ear



Exercise:

Say and spell the words:

1. head _______
2. leg _______
3. foot _______


End of Unit Assessment

Answer the questions:

1. Name 3 clothes: ______________________
2. Point to your: head / eyes / leg
3. Name 3 parts of your body: ___________



Activity:
Point to clothes and body parts with your partner.
