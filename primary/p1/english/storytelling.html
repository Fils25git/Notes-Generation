UNIT 10: STORYTELLING

Lesson 10.1: End of Unit Assessment

We practise listening, speaking, and telling simple stories.


Short dialogue:

Teacher: Listen to the story carefully.
Child: Yes, teacher.

Teacher: Who is in the story?
Child: A cat and a dog.

Teacher: What did the cat do?
Child: The cat drank milk.

Exercise:

1. Name the characters in the story: _______

2. Say what the cat did: _______

3. Say what the dog did: _______

4. Match the character with the action:

CharacterAction
CatDrank milk
DogRan in the yard

5. Retell the story using your own words: _______

Activity:

1. Draw a picture of your favourite character from the story.
2. Tell a short story about your picture to your partner.
3. Ask your partner questions about their story.
